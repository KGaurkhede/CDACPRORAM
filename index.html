CDAC

C-DAC Thiruvananthapuram (formerly ER&DCI), is a pioneer in application oriented research, design,
development and Technology Transfer in the areas of Electronics and Information Technology. Over the years, the Centre has acquired high competencyexpertise and experience in Control & Instrumentation, Networking, Broadcast & Communications, Power Electronics, VLSI/ ASIC Design and Underwater Electronics, with extensive design and deployment of software meta-resources for the projects.

The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing, C-DAC with the merger of four societies (ER&DCI, CEDT, NCST, C-DAC) has emerged as a super player in Information Technology (IT) industry, nationally and internationally. Research and Development (R&D), Education and Training are the two major areas of focus which have resulted in development of quality products such as high computing facility open source software, multilingual tools, training courses etc. C-DAC has grown in strength with Resource Persons and Resource Centres located at its Centres. With these resources networked, the Centre is advancing in development of technologies and building of InfoTech Skills at the speed of innovation. STDC provides Training Programmes,Inplant Training,Student Project & ACETC Franchise.
